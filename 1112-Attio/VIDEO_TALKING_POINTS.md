# Video Talking Points for Attio Application

## Video Structure (60-90 seconds total)

### Opening Hook (25-30 seconds)
**What to say:**
"Hey Attio team, I'm Ben. I applied for this role about a year ago and you told me I wasn't technical enough. Fair feedback. So I built an iOS app from scratch using AI prompt engineering. Not because I'm a programmer, but because I wanted to prove I could handle the technical side of support operations at an AI-native company like yours."

**Why this works:**
- Acknowledges the elephant in the room immediately
- Shows growth and response to feedback
- Demonstrates initiative and technical capability
- AI prompt engineering is directly relevant to an AI-native CRM

---

### Body: Your Unique Fit (35-40 seconds)

**Point 1: AI Implementation Experience (15-20 seconds)**
"When you raised $52M from Google Ventures in August and announced you're at 5,000 customers with 4x ARR growth, I knew this was the moment you'd need someone who's actually been in the trenches of AI support implementation. I launched Intercom's Fin AI bot when it was still in alpha at Kit.com. It saved 400 hours a month, but more importantly, I learned what actually works when you're balancing AI automation with human support."

**Point 2: Operations at Scale (15-20 seconds)**
"I've done the hard part of scaling support operations at a B2B SaaS company. I took Kit.com from 72-hour response times to under 5 minutes while growing the team 300%. I know what breaks when you scale fast, and I know how to build systems that don't."

**Why this works:**
- References specific, recent company news (shows you're following them)
- Ties your experience directly to their current growth stage
- Demonstrates understanding of AI-native support challenges
- Shows you understand their pain points

---

### Closing (15 seconds)
"I know the role. I know the tools. And this time, I have the technical chops you were looking for. Let's talk about how I can help Attio's support operations become a competitive advantage during this growth phase."

**Why this works:**
- Confident but not arrogant
- Clear call to action
- Reinforces that you've addressed their previous concern
- Ties back to business value

## Technical Setup Tips

- **Lighting:** Face a window or use a ring light
- **Audio:** Use headphones with a mic or a decent USB microphone
- **Background:** Clean, professional, nothing distracting
- **Camera angle:** Eye level, not looking down at laptop
- **Framing:** Head and shoulders visible, not too close
- **Energy:** Sit up straight, smile naturally, be conversational
- **Takes:** Record 3-5 takes and pick the best one
- **Length:** Keep it under 90 seconds (60-75 is ideal)
